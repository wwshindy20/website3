Poison Dart Frogs

The Size of the Frogs

The Frogs have a varying size from the size of a pinky nail, to the size of about a skotch tape dispenser. From smallest to largest the Species are

  • Ranitomeya (smallest)
  • Oophaga
  • Epidobates
  • Galactonotus
  • Phyllobates
  • Ameerega
  • Dendrobates (largest)

    • The Habitat

    The frogs live in seclusion from society in the temperate tropical rainforest of the Amazon. They live in southern Centeral America, and Northern South America. They are extremely sensitive to temperature. Where they live the temperature stays from 66F-78F and is at a constant humidity of at least 70%.
